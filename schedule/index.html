Schedule Digitize Iași Hackathon

  • 08:40 - 09:00: Check-in & welcome coffee

    Getting ready for the hackathon, making sure everybody has all they need for a productive day

  • 09:00 - 09:15: Hackathon kickoff

    Welcoming message from organizers and event partners

  • 09:15 - 09:45: Presenting hackathon challenges

    Partners will be presenting their proposed challenges and answer questions from participants

  • 09:45 - 12:00: First coding session

    Teams and individuals will start crafting their ideas, with guidance from mentors

  • 12:00 - 12:40: Lunch

    Will take a small break for lunch, recharging our batteries

  • 12:40 - 16:00: Second coding session

    Hackathon participants gather for the second coding session

  • 16:00 - 17:00: Time for demo

    After 5+ hours of coding, participants will pitch their ideas and answer questions from the jury members

  • 17:00 - 18:00: Selecting winners

    Jury members will select winners based on the impact, business value of the solution and how realistic is to be applied in Iași
